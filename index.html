EzLift

Hi, I'm vinci. I've been lifting weights for several years and have always relied on a note-taking app to track my progress.

After testing numerous apps, I found that they were often overloaded with excessive information, lacked user-friendliness, were time-consuming to use, or restricted the flexibility of entering set, weight, and reps.

To address these issues, I developed my own app. I hope you find it useful as well, and I welcome any feedback or suggestions you may have.
